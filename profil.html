Faculties available at Daraya University
Business School is an educational
institution that focuses on preparing
students for prestigious positions
in the labor market. It offers a bachelor's

A dental school is an institution
of higher education dedicated to training students
to become professional dentists. It offers a
comprehensive program of dental education covering

clinical dentistry.
The Faculty of Physiotherapy at Derayah

profession of pharmacy in the future.

Deraya University is committed to providing a high-quality education service to modern higher education in Egypt in general, and in Upper Egypt in particular, with an emphasis on active intellectual participation, research, and exploration, all within a unique and unprecedented learning environment.
The university always seeks to support the general benefit of society through research and education activities. We strive to be the best in everything we deliver, with a team and professional environment of competencies in all disciplines with the highest professional ethics to lead and support change in our community.
We continuously strive to be among the best in all we do Teaching, Research, Social Responsibility, and service to each other in our daily interactions.
